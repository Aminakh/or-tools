Detailed Description

MakeInactiveOperator.

MakeInactiveOperator makes path nodes inactive. Possible neighbors for the path 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4 (where 1 and 4 are first and last nodes of the path) are: 1 -> 3 -> 4 & 2 inactive 1 -> 2 -> 4 & 3 inactive
