Detailed Description

NestedSolve decision wrapper This decision calls a nested Solve on the given DecisionBuilder in its left branch; does nothing in the left branch.

The state of the decision corresponds to the result of the nested Solve: DECISION_PENDING - Nested Solve not called yet DECISION_FAILED - Nested Solve failed DECISION_FOUND - Nested Solve succeeded
