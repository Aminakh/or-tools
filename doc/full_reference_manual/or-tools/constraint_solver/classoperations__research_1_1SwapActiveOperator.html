Detailed Description

SwapActiveOperator.

SwapActiveOperator replaces an active node by an inactive one. Possible neighbors for the path 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4 with 5 inactive (where 1 and 4 are first and last nodes of the path) are: 1 -> 5 -> 3 -> 4 & 2 inactive 1 -> 2 -> 5 -> 4 & 3 inactive
